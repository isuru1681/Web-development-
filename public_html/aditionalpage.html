History about Dj

The Dj is an unforgettable item in a dance party or club. The past here stretches back to 1943. The first owner here is Jimmy Savile, He launched the world-first Dj dance party upstairs function room of the Loyal Order of Ancient Shepherds in Otley, England. In 1958 he became a radio Dj at Radio Luxembourg.

At the present day, the new technology has introduced modern sound systems new Dj styles. Such a new technology has made a whole new culture of Dj.
